# Dinner With Friends — Character Study Guide

A mobile-optimised character study guide for the Blue Ridge Community Theatre production of *Dinner With Friends* by Donald Margulies (Pulitzer Prize for Drama, 2000).

**Live prototype:** https://dripjobsjeremy.github.io/dinner-with-friends-character-study/

## Characters covered
- **Gabe** — food writer, husband, Tom's college friend
- **Karen** — food writer, wife, Beth's close friend

## Contents
- Play overview & what Margulies is really writing about
- Scene-by-scene character arcs
- Core needs, fears, and blind spots
- Questions for table work and early rehearsal
- Notes from professional productions & critics
- Craft methodology (Meisner, Stanislavski, subtext)
- Play structure quick reference
